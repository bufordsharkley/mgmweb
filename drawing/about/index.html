about the drawings...

These drawings are all on notebook paper, drawn almost exclusively with ballpoint pens. Ballpoint pens really are the best.

Many (probably a wide majority) were drawn while in class; they provided a great distraction from actually paying attention, or something.

There have been about 150 finished, finished drawings between 2001 and now. Many, many sketches exist. I'll be adding them soon.
