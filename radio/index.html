radio plays…

Acting/radio dramas with friends: enjoyed by many at the station. Hypothetically enjoyed by listeners.

Such bouts of radio dramas (scripts culled mainly from the incredible resource The Generic Radio Workshop) have been happening off and on since early 2012.

A history:

  • Our American Cousin, and a full season of weekly radio plays. Via the website of Nick&Noah (former KZSU royalty).
  • Two radio plays: a sitcom and a soap. Featuring acting from Hervey Okkles's own ma and sister.
  • A six-hour mini-marathon, January 2014.
